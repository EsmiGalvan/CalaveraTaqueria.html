Meet Calavera Taqueria

At Calavera Taqueria, we’re passionate about bringing bold, authentic flavors to your event. Based in Caldwell, Idaho, we specialize in crafting delicious tacos and catering experiences that make every occasion memorable. Our menu features a variety of options, from our tender Carnitas to our signature Pollo Calavera — a unique twist on chicken. Every dish is made with fresh, high-quality ingredients and cooked to perfection.

Whether you're hosting a small gathering or a large celebration, we provide personalized catering solutions to suit your needs. With a focus on exceptional food and excellent service, we aim to exceed your expectations and make your event one to remember. Reach out for a quote for your event and let us bring the flavor to your next celebration!
